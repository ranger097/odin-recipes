Chocolate Berry Tacos

These Chocolate-Berry Delight Dessert Tacos offer a playful twist
on the traditional taco concept. The warm and crispy cinnamon-sugar
shells provide a delightful contrast to the cool, refreshing fruit filling.
The drizzle of melted chocolate adds a decadent touch that ties everything
together in a sweet harmony. Whether you're enjoying them with friends or
indulging in a solo treat, these dessert tacos are sure to bring smiles and satisfaction.


Ingredients


For the Taco Shells:

  • 8 small flour tortillas

  • 2 tablespoons melted butter

  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

For the Filling:

  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)

  • 1/2 cup chopped bananas

  • 1/2 cup chopped mango

  • 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips

  • Whipped cream, for topping

For the Chocolate Drizzle:

  • 1/4 cup chocolate chips

  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream

Instructions:


  • Prepare the Taco Shells: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    Brush each flour tortilla with melted butter on both sides.
    In a small bowl, mix granulated sugar and ground cinnamon.
    Sprinkle the mixture over the buttered tortillas.
    Gently fold each tortilla in half and place them in between the
    grates of your oven rack, creating a taco shape. Bake for about 8-10
    minutes until they're golden and crispy. Allow them to cool before filling.

  • Prepare the Filling: In a bowl, mix the mixed berries, chopped bananas,
    and chopped mango. Toss in the mini chocolate chips for an added touch of
    sweetness and texture.

  • Make the Chocolate Drizzle: In a microwave-safe bowl,
    combine the chocolate chips and heavy cream. Microwave in 20-second intervals,
    stirring after each interval, until the chocolate is melted and the mixture is smooth.

  • Assemble the Dessert Tacos: Gently open each cooled taco shell.
    Spoon the mixed berry and fruit mixture into the taco shells.
    Drizzle the melted chocolate over the filling.

  • Top and Serve: Finish off your dessert tacos with a dollop of whipped cream
    on top of each taco. Serve immediately and enjoy this delightful combination of flavors and textures.
